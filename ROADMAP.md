# Roadmap

This roadmap favors durable, reproducible foundations over premature broad
claims.

## Milestone 1: Reproducible 3-SAT Baseline

Define the accepted 3-SAT input model; provide a small trusted verifier and
reference solvers; and record repeatable small-instance experiments with their
commands, versions, provenance, and finite-domain limits.

## Milestone 2: Literature and Known-Barrier Map

Curate primary and authoritative sources, known algorithms, reductions, and
proof barriers. Connect each research direction to relevant prior work and
document open limitations.

## Milestone 3: Reviewed Hypothesis and Counterexample Pipeline

Use structured hypotheses, explicit falsification conditions, counterexample
searches, reviewable proof dependencies, and preserved negative results so
that objections and revisions remain public.
